Citation
========


If you have used data from the **Kun** simulation suite, you may consider citing the `CSST Emulator paper <https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2025arXiv250211160C/abstract>`_:

.. code-block:: bibtex

    @ARTICLE{2025SCPMA..6889512C,
            author = {{Chen}, Zhao and {Yu}, Yu and {Han}, Jiaxin and {Jing}, Yipeng},
                title = "{CSST cosmological emulator I: Matter power spectrum emulation with one percent accuracy to k = 10h Mpc$^{{\ensuremath{-}}1}$}",
            journal = {Science China Physics, Mechanics, and Astronomy},
            keywords = {simulation, large-scale structure of the Universe, cosmology, Cosmology and Nongalactic Astrophysics},
                year = 2025,
                month = aug,
            volume = {68},
            number = {8},
                eid = {289512},
                pages = {289512},
                doi = {10.1007/s11433-025-2671-0},
        archivePrefix = {arXiv},
            eprint = {2502.11160},
        primaryClass = {astro-ph.CO},
            adsurl = {https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2025SCPMA..6889512C},
            adsnote = {Provided by the SAO/NASA Astrophysics Data System}
        }

    @ARTICLE{2024MNRAS.534.1205C,
